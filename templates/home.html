Here at Pedicoders we believe women are unstoppable.

Identifying as a woman brings a new and innovative perspective to a currently male dominated industry. Despite common misconceptions, women make excellent coders. In fact, there is some suggestion women might even be better coders than men *gasp*. But women only get this recognition of being an excellent coder if they keep one single piece of information hidden: their gender. We think it's time to change this. So pull up a chair with your girlfriends and get coding. Whether you're a complete beginner, or seasoned professional, we want to hear from you all.
